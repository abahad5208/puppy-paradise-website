About Dr. Patrona

Dr. Patrona was born in 1940 in Germany, and immigrated to America with her parents shortly after WW-II in 1948. She displayed a strong affinity for animals from a tender age, and became the first German-born veterinarian in the USA in 1965.

Over the course of her illustrious, 55-year career, she has published multiple books, including the bestselling Guide to Canine Behaviour and Care, and has guest-starred in countless talk shows, podcasts, radio programs, etc.

Dr. Patrona has a special place in her heart for our canine companions, and has travelled the world extensively to document the various forms and behaviours of man's best friend. As a result, she has amassed a plethora of knowledge and photographs, which she is eager to share with the world.

This website was created on behalf of Dr. Patrona, in the hope that current and future generations of fellow dog-lovers can benefit from her accomplishments. The photographs and information on this site are all from the notes and collection of Dr. Patrona herself.
