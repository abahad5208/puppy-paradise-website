The herding dog breed group includes Sheepdogs, Cattle Dogs, Livestock Dogs and Drovers.

The Hound Dog Breeds Group is comprised of the Scenthound & Sighthound Dog Breed Groups.

The Molossoid or Mastiff dog breed group are dogs used during ancient times to protect, defend and fight.

The Non-Sporting, Large Companion Dog Breeds group are dogs of every type and size that in the past had some utilitarian use to humans but no longer. Many of the larger dogs in this breed group fit into some other dog breed group as well based on their physical traits or past use.

The Primitive - Pariah Dog Breeds group consists of those dogs with little to no particular utilitarian use and considered in ancient times as scavengers.

The Toy - Small Companion Dog Breeds group consists of dogs with varied looks but have in common their small or 'lap' size. Any utilitarian use in the past has waned as they were bred only to be companions to humans.
